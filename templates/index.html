Hello!

Type in an address


e.g. Stanford University; Rodeo Drive, Los Angeles; Tallinn, Estonia; Hong Kong.

What happen? This draft app, a quickie modification of Ben Welsh's First News App, takes a given address, retrieves its coordinates from the Google Maps Geocoder, then passes those coordinates to Weather Underground and Instagram to find local weather and nearby interesting photos, respectively. It also makes a generic call to USGS for "Significant Earthquakes" worldwide in the past 30 days.

See the code on Github. This is my first Flask app and was meant as a quick demo and should not be used as an example of best practices or any practices.

Note: If there's an error in processing your address, it's probably because I hit a rate-limit or one of the APIs is down. Here's what the app results look like ideally.
